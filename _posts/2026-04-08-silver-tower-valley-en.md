A public tour route through Silver Tower Valley, in the western reaches of the ICD, has been opened as of today.

## Overview

The valley is filled with countless cylindrical silver towers, arranged in tight and orderly intervals. Each tower bears a crosswise or fissure-like marking near its summit. To the approaching visitor, the formation suggests both a vast sealed installation and a silent assembly of ritual spires.

The complex is believed to remain operational. At regular intervals, the air between the towers is observed to grow faintly taut, and something appears to be discharged outward into the distance. Across the entire landscape one is left with a singular impression: that what has been gathered is being quietly released.

## Highlights

- The disciplined formation of the silver towers
- Cyclical shifts in atmosphere within the valley
- Markings on the upper tower sections (binoculars recommended)

## Visitor Notice

As the site is presumed to remain active, departure from the designated route is strictly prohibited. Contact with or defacement of the towers carries severe penalties.
